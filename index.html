About Me

I'm an innovative Mechanical Engineer and Computational Scientist with over 4 years of experience in computational methods, modeling and high-performance computing (HPC). I hold a Dr. sc. in Computational Fluid Dynamics from ETH Zürich and specialize in developing simulation models, optimizing engineering processes and improving model accuracy for complex dynamical systems.

Currently working as a Postdoctoral Researcher at ETH Zürich, I focus on designing cost-efficient data assimilation frameworks for unsteady simulations of turbulent flows. My research has achieved accuracy improvements by a factor of 10 in mean flow, vortex shedding frequency and turbulent properties. I have extensive experience in supervising research projects and working in international, interdisciplinary teams.
